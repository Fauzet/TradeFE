How To Begin

So you came up to the homepage and see the intesertingly white button, you clicked on it. Suddenly you're redirected to a page greeting you with a login form. You did not know where is the registration button, it is just a form with two inputs. Here is the explanation, our platform did not complicate you with the 'ol registration form. We are not curious with your phone numbers and addresses. When you submit the form, it will automatically check if the combination of username and password exists in our database, if yes, then it will log you into your account. Otherwise, it will create one for you and you get logged in.

Next, you'll be depositing your asset into your account. To do this, you can click the deposit tab on your sidebar (as shown below)

Then, you need a FaucetPay account. I'm not gonna expand what FaucetPay is, but FaucetPay is one of the largest microwallet service, bridging more than thousands of faucet worldwide. If you don't have an account, you can make one here

After finished the registration on FaucetPay, you can deposit some coins to there and go back to our deposit page. First, you need to select your desired cryptocurrency to deposit. Secondly, put the amount you want to deposit. Finally, click "Deposit".

Actually, there is one more input we haven't discussed. The "Deposit With" input. This input field is one shortcut for you to deposit one currency and receive other currency without the need to exchange them and pay trading fee. We call this "Swap Deposit". When you select one currency on it, you'll be charged with that specific cryptocurrency instead of the target currency (the first input)
